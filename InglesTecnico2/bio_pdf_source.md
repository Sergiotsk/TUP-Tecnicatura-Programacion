  <h1>Sergio Tschernitschek</h1>
  <div class="meta">Backend Developer &nbsp;·&nbsp; TUP Student &nbsp;·&nbsp; UTN Argentina</div>

<hr>

**Sergio Tschernitschek** is a student of the *Tecnicatura Universitaria en Programación* (TUP) at the Universidad Tecnológica Nacional (UTN) in Argentina, specializing in backend development and software automation. With over 15 years of professional experience as an electrician and PC technician, Sergio brings a rare combination of hands-on technical expertise and programming knowledge to every project he undertakes.

His background spans hardware diagnostics, electrical systems, and direct client support — skills that have built a strong foundation for understanding complex technical problems from the ground up. Currently, Sergio is expanding his expertise in **Go**, **Docker**, **WebSockets**, and **AI API integration**, with a focus on building high-performance backend systems.

Among his most significant achievements is the development of **TalkGo**, a real-time audio translation backend built in Go. The system receives audio via WebSocket, transcribes it using OpenAI's Whisper API, and delivers the translated text back to the client in under three seconds — all handled concurrently using Go's native goroutines and the hub pattern. The project is fully containerized with Docker and available on GitHub.

His long-term goal is to become a specialist in backend development and IoT systems, bridging the gap between physical infrastructure and modern software solutions. Sergio is committed to continuous, practical learning and applies every concept he studies to real, working projects.
